About Us

  •

    Vision

    To be a leading Tax Administration that significantly contributes to the social and economic well-being of the people of Belize.

  •

    Mission

    Through a highly skilled cadre of professionals, administer the relevant tax laws in an equitable and fair manner to promote the highest levels of voluntary compliance and achieve the maximum degree of public confidence in the Belize Tax Service (BTS).

  •

    Core Values

    The core values resonate the image of the BTS and promote its new culture. Core values also outline the personal values that management and employees should display in their daily work ethics and interactions with all stakeholders. Aligning these values into an easily memorable acronym aids stakeholders and BTS employees in effortlessly recalling and integrating them into their daily routines and business delivery activities.

  • PRIDE

    Professionalism, Respect, Integrity, Driven, Excellence
